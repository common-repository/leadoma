== Description ==
Leadoma is a powerful CRM plugin for WordPress that can help you identify your most valuable customers, pinpoint those who are leaving you for your competitors, and ultimately maximize profits from each customer. 
With our marketing automation services, you can easily automate your campaigns without having to write any code or rely on data scientists. But that's not all – our customer insight and analytics tools allow you to visualize and manage your customer data and community, giving you unparalleled insights into what your customers need, even if they don't tell you directly. 
So why wait? Start using Leadoma today to take your business to the next level!

We developed an [api](https://api.leadoma.com/docs) that utilizes the Formaloo API. You can find the [full documentation](https://docs.formaloo.net/#/) here, and for additional information, refer to the [developer resources](https://help.formaloo.com/en/collections/3330828-for-developers)

== Installation ==
1. Upload `leadoma.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Login/Register on plugin
4. Wait for customers to join
